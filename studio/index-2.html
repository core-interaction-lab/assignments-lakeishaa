Lakeisha Esthetique Sharonina                                                Project 1: Cross-Platform Storytelling


This project explores the narrative story of Steve who is studying abroad in Indonesia and decides to learn the langauge. He tries watching Indonesian movies, starts online lessons, actively speaking with the locals and listens to Indonesian music. As Steve was listening to the music, he wondered what the lyrics meant.
