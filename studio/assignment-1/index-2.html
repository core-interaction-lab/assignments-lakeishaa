Lakeisha Esthetique Sharonina                                             Project 1: Cross-Platform Storytelling


This project explores the narrative story of Steve who is studying abroad in Indonesia and decides to learn the language. He starts online lessons, actively speaks with the locals, watches Indonesian movies and listens to Indonesian music. As Steve was scrolling through Instagram he found an ad for a local band. As he listens to their music, he wonders what the lyrics mean.
